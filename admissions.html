Admission Requirements

  • Child must be at least 5 years old
  • Ability to read Arabic letters (basic)
  • Parent/Guardian commitment to daily attendance
  • Completed registration form
  • One passport photograph

Registration Process

  1. Obtain and complete the admission form
  2. Submit form with required documents
  3. Attend assessment/interview
  4. Receive admission offer
  5. Pay fees and begin classes
