Our Programmes

Comprehensive Islamic education for young Muslims

Qur'an Memorization (Hifdh)

Structured daily memorization with qualified Huffaz teachers guiding students through the complete memorization of the Holy Qur'an.

Tajweed Studies

Comprehensive study of Tajweed rules including Makharij, Sifaat, and practical application in Qur'an recitation.

Islamic Studies

Fiqh, Aqeedah, Seerah, and Akhlaq taught in an age-appropriate manner to build strong Islamic foundation.

Reading, writing, and comprehension of Arabic to enable students understand the Qur'an directly.

Hadith Studies

Memorization and understanding of selected Ahadith from authentic collections.

Character Development

Moral and ethical training to develop upright character and good manners.
