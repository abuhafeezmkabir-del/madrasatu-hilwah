About Our School

Learn about our journey, mission, and commitment to Qur'an memorization

Our Story

Masrasatu Hulwatu Tahfeezil Qur'an was established in 2026 under the noble guidance of JIBWIS Idah, Kogi State Branch. Located at Hajiya Khadijat Amodu's Centre for Waqf, Angwa, Idah, Kogi State, our school was founded with a clear vision: to build a generation of upright, disciplined Qur'an memorizers.

We are committed to providing a simplified, organized, and structured Qur'an memorization program with defined schedules for Hifdh and related Islamic texts. Our students learn under qualified teachers who emphasize correct Tajweed, good character, and deep understanding of the Qur'an.

Our Objectives
  • To produce Hafiz/Hafizah with correct Tajweed
  • To instill Islamic character and discipline
  • To create a conducive learning environment
  • To serve as a center of Qur'anic excellence

JIBWIS Affiliation

Our school operates under the esteemed guidance and supervision of Jama'atu Izalatil Bid'ah Wa Iqamatis Sunnah (JIBWIS), Idah Branch, Kogi State. This affiliation ensures our adherence to authentic Islamic teachings and commitment to the Sunnah in all our educational activities.
