About Our School

Madrasatu Hilwah Tahfizil Qur'an

Established in 2026 under JIBWIS Idah, Kogi State Branch, Madrasatu Hilwah Tahfizil Qur'an is dedicated to building the next generation of Qur'an memorizers who embody the teachings of the Glorious Qur'an.

Located at Hajiya Khadijat Amodu's Centre for Waqf, Angwa, Idah, Kogi State, we provide a peaceful, structured environment where children develop into Hafiz/Hafizah with correct Tajweed and strong Islamic character.
